Vajram


Kannur: An innovative project to provide education not only to students but also to their parents has been started at Ilayavoor CHM Higher Secondary School.
In this scheme called Vajram, the educational qualification of their parents and their interest in further studies are ascertained from the students through a special survey, they are trained for SSLC, Plus Two and Degree courses and appear for the exam.
The children accepted it with the view that my parents should learn along with me. This activity will help the adults who could not continue their education due to various problems in their childhood to achieve a big dream in their life. Student learning
The idea of ​​such a social revolution arose from the school parliament's conclusion that the educational level of the parents also had a significant role to play in improving it. There is full support and assistance of school management, PTA and staff members in this regard.
Mr. Shaju John, District Coordinator of the Literacy Mission, inaugurated the orientation class of this project, which is being conducted in collaboration with the Literacy Mission. He shared with the learners
that learning has no age limit and anyone who is interested can learn in the light of the literacy mission work of the past several years.
PTA President Muhammadali Kudali presided. In the meeting, 60-year-old Najma Irakur, who achieved high marks in SSLC through further education, was honored. Regarding school manager PA Karim. Headmaster PP Zubair explained the scheme. Teachers Krishnakumar KM, Lata TO, Prakashan A, Mahmood PC
, Jabir and student parliamentarians Rana Razak PC, Fatima Rafa CCP and Shifa Naureen also spoke. The meeting also cleared the doubts of the parents of the students regarding the further course of study
